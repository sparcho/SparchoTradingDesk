Executive Summary

Persistent Systems is a digital-engineering IT-services company (BFSI, healthcare, tech) with an unusually consistent growth record — 24 straight quarters of sequential growth. FY26: revenue $1,654.4M (+17.4% USD) / ₹14,748 Cr (+23.5% INR), EBIT margin 15.6%; Q4 PAT ₹529 Cr (+33.7%); strong order booking (Q4 TCV $600.8M, ACV $445.1M) (persistent/multibagg, FY26). FY26 PAT ~₹1,656 Cr (PE-implied; exact FY PENDING). Market cap ₹77,140 Cr at ~46.6× P/E; promoter (founder Anand Deshpande) 29.35%.

CORE VERDICT. Persistent is the best grower in the IT batch — 24 consecutive quarters of growth, +23.5% INR revenue in FY26, strong TCV, and a credible AI-led digital-engineering pivot. It's a high-quality, net-cash compounder. The honest caveats: (1) it trades at a premium ~46.6× PE (a growth multiple that needs the growth to continue); (2) margins are mid-tier (~15.6% EBIT) — scaling but below top-tier IT; (3) sub-50% promoter (29.35%). The load-bearing question is whether the growth + AI pivot sustain the ~20%+ pace to justify the multiple. High-quality consistent grower; the premium is the only real constraint. The pick of the IT batch on growth quality.
