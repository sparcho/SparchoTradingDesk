Executive Summary

Ceinsys Tech (rebranding to CS Tech AI) is a GIS/geospatial + engineering + tech-solutions small-cap. FY26 was strong: consolidated revenue ₹660.7 Cr (+58%), PAT ₹133.4 Cr (+111%), EBITDA ₹144.9 Cr (+85.8%, 21.9% margin), EPS ₹67.9 (whalesbook/tradingview, FY26). Promoter holding has declined over 3 years; the company is rebranding (CS Tech AI) + reducing government-business reliance.

CORE VERDICT. Ceinsys is a cheap, fast-growing, net-cash small-cap — +58% revenue / +111% PAT, 21.9% EBITDA, an ₹876 Cr order book (>1× revenue), net cash, at a ~13× PE (among the cheapest-with-growth in the whole program). The AI/tech-solutions pivot + GIS heritage are credible. The honest caveats: (1) declining promoter holding over 3 years (a governance watch); (2) small-cap (₹1,728 Cr) liquidity/scale risk; (3) historical government-business reliance (which it's reducing); (4) a Q4 "profit slip" noted despite the strong FY. The load-bearing question is whether the growth + AI-pivot sustain while the promoter-holding trend + govt-dependence are managed. Cheap + high-growth small-cap; declining promoter + small-size are the constraints. One of the more attractive value-growth combinations in Batches 8-9 — for those comfortable with small-cap + the promoter-trend watch.
