0. Why this frame, why now

Jensen's framing (Energy → Chips → Infra → Models → Applications) is a supply-chain map: demand cascades DOWN from the app layer and the binding constraint is UP at energy. The US-centric naming on his list (NVDA / TSM / AMZN / OpenAI / Palantir) is mostly inaccessible to a SPARCHO-HDFC book that trades NSE.

What Indian equity markets express well across the cake:

  • Layer 1 Energy — India's power-transition is active (PLI solar, grid buildout, nuclear at the edges). Multiple listed plays.
  • Layer 2 Chips — limited direct-fab exposure; India's hook is OSAT + EMS (Kaynes, Dixon, Syrma) — early-stage but policy-supported.
  • Layer 3 Infrastructure — telecom fiber (HFCL/STLTECH/TEJAS) + cabling (POLYCAB/KEI) + cooling (BLUESTAR) + DC-adjacent (ANANT RAJ, NETWEB). Strongest physical-infra exposure globally outside US/China.
  • Layer 4 Models — India is services-layer, not frontier-model-layer. TCS / INFY / HCLTECH / PERSISTENT monetise AI as consulting and integration work. No pure Indian LLM yet listed (Sarvam, Krutrim, BharatGPT all private).
  • Layer 5 Applications — rich mid-cap vertical AI (KPIT automotive, NAUKRI hiring, MAPMYINDIA location, NEWGEN workflow, TANLA CPaaS).

What the US-centric frame MISSES and India expresses uniquely — see §7 below.

Binding constraint. Data-centre + EV + industrial electrification all pull through here. Jensen's line "China has 2x our energy capacity" lands differently in India: we are a net electricity importer in many states, grid capacity is the choke point, not generation capacity.

6. SILVER / COMMODITY BRIDGE TO AI

Often missed in the 5LC frame. Operator already exposes this via silver complex (XAG / SILVERBEES) + minerals cluster (MOIL / GMDC / HINDZINC).

Mechanisms:

  • Silver in solar panels — TOPCon and HJT cells use 15-20 mg silver per watt. Global solar buildout = structural silver demand. Already a core part of operator's XAG thesis per CLAUDE.md §14.5 cup-and-handle framework.
  • Copper in grid + chip packaging — HINDCOPPER (Core watchlist candidate), VEDL (already in §16).
  • Zinc + lead in batteries — HINDZINC (already in §16).
  • Rare earths / critical minerals in EV motors — GMDC (already in §16) via Gujarat concessions.

This bridge means operator's minerals cluster IS part of the AI thesis — it just enters via Layer 1 (energy/storage) rather than Layer 2 (chips). This was already captured in F23 ("minerals concentration is deliberate sector bet") — the 5LC frame now makes the connection explicit.
