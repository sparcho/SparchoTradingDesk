Executive Summary

Hindustan Aeronautics is India's defence-aerospace national champion — fighter jets (Tejas/LCA), helicopters, trainers, engines + MRO. FY26: revenue ₹33,089 Cr (+7%), net profit ₹9,116 Cr (+9%); Q4 revenue ₹13,942 Cr (+2%), Q4 PAT ₹4,196 Cr (+5.5%) (upstox/marketsmojo, FY26). Order book ₹2.54 lakh cr (31-Mar-26, up from a ₹1.89 lakh cr opening) — ~7.7× sales, the largest OB-to-sales visibility in the program. GoI 71.6%; market cap ₹3,09,790 Cr at ~34× P/E. FY27 guidance: 10-12% revenue growth (LCA Mk1A + HTT-40 deliveries), despite supply-chain/execution risks.

CORE VERDICT. HAL is a top-tier "assurance from the nature of the work" name — India's near-monopoly defence-aerospace manufacturer with ~27.5% PAT margins, net cash, high ROE, and a ₹2.54 lakh cr order book (~7.7× sales) that underwrites years of revenue. Quality-wise it sits alongside BEL as the cream of the defence PSUs. The honest caveats: (1) near-term growth is execution-paced — FY26 revenue grew only +7% (Q4 +2%), gated by LCA-Mk1A engine-supply (GE) + execution constraints; (2) it's a premium multiple (~34× PE / ~9.4× sales); (3) govt-pricing/budget dependence. The load-bearing question is whether the huge OB converts faster (engine supply unblocking → FY27 10-12% guide) to grow into the multiple. Best-in-class defence-aerospace monopoly; premium valuation + supply-chain-paced growth are the constraints.

Section 3 / The Aerospace-Monopoly USP — Durable Compounder or Execution-Gated?

Variant-perception crux. Consensus: the defence-aerospace monopoly on the indigenisation supercycle, with a vast OB. Variant question: how fast does the ₹2.54 lakh cr OB convert given engine-supply/execution constraints?

3.1 Why the USP is real

Sovereign-critical, near-monopoly aircraft design/build/MRO + a vast OB + indigenisation (Tejas/AMCA pipeline) + high-margin MRO annuity = a durable, hard-to-replicate franchise.

3.2 Honest scepticism — five open questions

  1. Engine supply. LCA-Mk1A GE-engine supply — the delivery gate; resolved?
  2. Growth pace. FY26 +7% — FY27 10-12% guide depends on execution unblocking.
  3. Valuation. ~34× PE / ~9.4× sales — premium.
  4. Govt-pricing. PSU dependence on MoD budget/pricing.
  5. AMCA/future programmes. Long-dated upside (5th-gen fighter).
USP VERDICT. A best-in-class monopoly franchise — the investment question is OB-conversion pace (engine supply/execution) + the premium multiple, not the franchise.
