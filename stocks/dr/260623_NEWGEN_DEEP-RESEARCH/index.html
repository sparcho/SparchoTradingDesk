Executive Summary

Newgen Software is a low-code platform company (NewgenONE: BPM + ECM + customer-journey) for banks, insurers, and government, transitioning to SaaS + AI. FY26: revenue ₹1,574 Cr (+6% YoY), PAT ₹301 Cr (₹42 Cr exceptional; adjusted PAT ₹334 Cr, +6%) (newgensoft/whalesbook, FY26). Subscription revenue ₹525 Cr (+24%), SaaS +36%, annuity now 62% of revenue (from 56%); 47 new logos; >₹5 Cr customers 87→101. The honest headline: the stock is down ~58% over the past year — overall revenue growth slowed to +6% (India/Middle-East large-deal delays) even as the subscription mix improved. Market cap ₹6,705 Cr at ~20-22× P/E (cheapest across these batches); promoter 53.5%, net cash.

CORE VERDICT. Newgen has the cleanest fundamental screen across all five batches — majority promoter (53.5%), net cash, high software margins/ROCE, and a P/E near the ≤20 threshold (~20-22×) — a genuine rarity in this universe of premium names. But the cheapness is earned, not free: FY26 revenue grew only +6% (large-deal delays in India/ME), which is why the stock de-rated ~58%. The bull thesis is the SaaS transition (subscription +24%, annuity 62%) + AI products (Marvin/Lumyn) re-accelerating growth on a now-reasonable multiple. The load-bearing question is whether growth re-accelerates from +6% toward the historical ~20%. The value + quality screen is the most attractive in the program; the growth slowdown is the real risk.
