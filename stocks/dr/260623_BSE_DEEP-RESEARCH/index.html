Executive Summary

BSE Limited is India's #2 stock exchange — an asset-light, transaction-fee duopoly (with NSE) whose FY26 was explosive on the Sensex derivatives (F&O) boom: revenue from operations ₹4,834 Cr (+63.5%), consolidated total income ₹5,148 Cr, EBITDA ₹3,393 Cr (48% margin), PAT ₹2,475 Cr (+87.9%), EPS ₹60.61 (business-standard/angelone/sahi, FY26 results 7-May-2026); derivatives revenue >2× to ₹3,134 Cr, equity-derivatives ADT ₹245 trillion in Q4. PBT margin expanded to 64.1% (from 54%). ₹10 dividend; stock +50.55% YTD-2026.

CORE VERDICT. BSE is a high-quality, asset-light exchange duopoly with extraordinary operating leverage (48% EBITDA, 64% PBT margin) and an explosive FY26 (+88% PAT) — genuine "assurance from the nature of the work" as a franchise. But the single dominant risk is that the growth is F&O-volume-led, and F&O is precisely what SEBI regulates (lot-size, expiry rationalisation, retail-suitability) — a regulatory action can reset derivatives volumes (and BSE's earnings) quickly. The load-bearing question is the durability of derivatives volumes against the regulatory backdrop. Premium valuation + regulatory-dependence are the constraints on a genuinely high-quality model.

Section 3 / The Exchange-Duopoly USP — Durable or F&O-Regulation-Exposed?

Variant-perception crux. Consensus: a re-rating exchange duopoly riding the derivatives boom + StAR MF + CDSL optionality. Variant question: how much of the +88% PAT is durable vs a regulation-vulnerable F&O spike?

3.1 Why the USP is real

An exchange is a regulated, asset-light, network-effect duopoly with huge operating leverage — structurally high-quality. BSE has genuinely gained derivatives share.

3.2 Honest scepticism — five open questions

  1. F&O regulation. SEBI lot-size/expiry/suitability actions can reset volumes — the #1 risk.
  2. Volume durability. ADT ₹245 trillion — sustainable or a euphoria peak?
  3. NSE competition. NSE remains dominant; NSE IPO could shift dynamics.
  4. Valuation. Premium multiple on a regulation-sensitive earnings spike.
  5. Mix. How much non-derivatives (StAR MF, listing, data) diversification?
USP VERDICT. Excellent franchise; the risk is that the recent earnings power is F&O-volume-led and regulation-exposed — durability is the question, not quality.
