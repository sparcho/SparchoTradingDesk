Executive Summary

Himadri Speciality Chemical is India's leading specialty-carbon / coal-tar company (carbon black, advanced carbon materials, pitch) now executing the "specialty-chem → battery storage" pivot the operator flagged: it commissioned its first Li-ion anode-material plant (Mahistikry, West Bengal, 23-Apr-2026, 200 MTPA initial, in-house tech) and holds battery-tech partnerships (IBC, Sicona). FY26 was a record: revenue ₹4,661 Cr, EBITDA ₹1,006 Cr (+19% from ₹847 Cr, ~21.6% margin), PAT ₹755 Cr (+36%); Q4 PAT ₹208 Cr (+29%) (scanx/univest/investywise, FY26 results). The balance sheet is net cash +₹121 Cr, and management guides to double PAT to ₹1,100 Cr by FY28. Market cap ₹29,168 Cr → ~38.6× P/E — rich in absolute terms but the cheapest in Batch 1, with a profitable cash-generative core underneath.

CORE VERDICT. Himadri is the highest-quality base business of Batch 1 — profitable (₹755 Cr PAT), net-cash, ~21.6% margins — plus a genuine import-substitution anode/battery-materials optionality that maps directly to the operator's "specialty-chem into battery storage" thesis. The honest caveats: (1) the anode plant is tiny today (200 MTPA initial) so the battery upside is optionality, not yet earnings; (2) ~38.6× PE / ~6.3× sales still bakes in execution. The load-bearing question is whether the anode/battery ramp + the FY28 ₹1,100 Cr PAT guide deliver, turning optionality into the multiple. The most "assurance from the nature of the work" name in the batch — real cash today, real battery wedge tomorrow.

Section 3 / The Anode / Battery-Materials Pivot — Real Wedge or Early Optionality?

Variant-perception crux. Consensus: a quality specialty-carbon compounder with a high-optionality battery-materials pivot. Variant question: how soon does the anode/battery line move from tiny (200 MTPA) to material earnings?

3.1 Why the wedge is real

Li-ion anode material is largely imported (China); a domestic, in-house-tech producer with an existing carbon platform + customer relationships is well-placed for import substitution as Indian gigafactories scale. IBC/Sicona partnerships add silicon-anode/next-gen exposure.

3.2 Honest scepticism — five open questions

  1. Scale. 200 MTPA initial is small — what's the ramp/capex/timeline to material capacity?
  2. Offtake. Which cell-makers commit to Himadri anode (offtake contracts)? PENDING.
  3. Economics. Anode margins vs the carbon-black core — accretive? PENDING.
  4. FY28 guide. Doubling PAT to ₹1,100 Cr by FY28 — core vs battery contribution split?
  5. Valuation. 38.6× PE already credits the optionality somewhat.
PIVOT VERDICT. Real and well-positioned, but early — today it is optionality on top of a solid cash core, not yet an earnings driver. The base business justifies a quality multiple; the battery wedge is the upside call.
