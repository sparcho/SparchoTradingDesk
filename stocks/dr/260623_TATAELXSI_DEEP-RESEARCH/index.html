Executive Summary

Tata Elxsi is a premium design + ER&D house (transportation/SDV, broadcast/OTT, healthcare). FY26: revenue ₹3,757.4 Cr, PAT ₹628 Cr, full-year PBT margin 23.4%; Q4 revenue ₹993.8 Cr, EBITDA margin 24.6%, PAT margin 21.1% (tataelxsi/investywise, FY26). Dividend ₹75/sh. Tata group holds 43.9%; market cap ₹25,327 Cr at ~40× P/E.

CORE VERDICT. Tata Elxsi is a genuinely high-quality, asset-light ER&D franchise — ~25% EBITDA margins, high ROCE, net cash, a strong OEM/SDV automotive position (the AI/chip-design-adjacent leg the operator has eyed in DR cross-briefs), and a healthcare/broadcast mix. The honest caveats: (1) growth has decelerated (FY26 revenue ~+6%, well below its historical 15-20%) on auto-ER&D + broadcast softness — the stock has already de-rated from its ~60-70× peak to ~40×; (2) sub-50% promoter (43.9%); (3) still a premium multiple for the current growth. The load-bearing question is whether SDV/auto-ER&D demand + AI re-accelerate growth back toward the high-teens. Premium quality; the decelerated growth vs the ~40× multiple is the constraint.
