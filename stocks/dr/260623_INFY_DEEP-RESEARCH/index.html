CORE VERDICT. Infosys is a high-quality #2 IT compounder — crossed $20B revenue, ~21% margins, ~30%+ ROCE, net cash, strong large-deal wins ($14.9bn) + FCF ($3.7bn), and a big cash-return (₹37,500 Cr). Growth (+3.1% CC) is better than TCS's FY26 (−2.4%) but still modest on the soft IT-demand backdrop, and the FY27 guide is cautious (1.5-3.5%). The honest constraints: (1) modest growth + cautious guidance; (2) sub-50% (widely held, ~13%) promoter; (3) ~21-22× PE (less cheap than TCS's ~15.6×). The load-bearing question is IT-demand recovery + AI (Topaz) re-accelerating growth. High-quality + reasonable-PE + cash-return; modest growth + widely-held + a touch pricier than TCS are the trade-offs. A quality IT name; TCS screens cheaper on PE.
