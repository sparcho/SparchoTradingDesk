Executive Summary

Info Edge runs Naukri (India's dominant online-recruitment monopoly, ~70%+ share) + 99acres + Jeevansathi + Shiksha, plus a large listed-investment portfolio (notably Zomato/Eternal + PB Fintech stakes). FY26: consolidated revenue ₹3,285 Cr (+15.3%), consolidated PAT ₹2,121.3 Cr (+9%) — the PAT includes large other-income/investment gains (Q4 other income ₹497 Cr); the operating core runs ~40% margins (Q4 operating profit +39.4%) (entrackr/upstox, FY26). Naukri Q4 ₹608 Cr; 99acres Q4 ₹144 Cr. Market cap ₹62,102 Cr (~29× PE) — but valuation is sum-of-the-parts (operating core + investment book). Dividend ₹3.60.

CORE VERDICT. Info Edge is a two-part story: (1) a high-quality operating core — Naukri is a ~70%-share recruitment monopoly with ~40% operating margins + net cash, a genuinely excellent cash-compounding classifieds franchise; plus (2) a large listed-investment portfolio (Zomato/Eternal, PB Fintech) that often accounts for a big share of the market cap. The honest framing: the raw screen (PE ~29×, MCap/Sales ~19×) is misleading because the market cap embeds the investment book — you must value it SOTP (core recruitment business + mark-to-market of the listed stakes). The load-bearing questions are (a) Naukri core growth (recruitment-cycle-sensitive), and (b) the value/volatility of the Zomato/PB stakes. High-quality monopoly core + an investment-holdco overlay; judge it as a SOTP, not on headline PE.
